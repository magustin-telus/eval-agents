# id: POL-RETAIL-BANKING
# title: Retail Banking Complaint Resolution Policy
# category: retail_banking

## Scope
Complaints about checking and savings accounts and everyday banking: account fees,
overdraft and NSF charges, deposit holds, failed or delayed transfers, debit card issues,
incorrect balances, account closures, and access or login problems.

## Investigation steps
1. Verify the customer's identity and identify the affected account and transaction(s).
2. Review the transaction ledger, fee schedule, and any holds applied.
3. Confirm whether fees or holds were applied in line with the account agreement.
4. For failed or delayed transfers, trace the transaction status and counterparties.
5. For access issues, confirm account standing and any security flags.

## Remediation
- Fees applied in error are refunded within 3 business days.
- Improper deposit holds are released and affected funds are made available promptly.
- Failed or duplicated transfers are reversed or completed within 5 business days.
- Debit card problems are resolved by reissuing the card within 7 business days where needed.
- A written confirmation of the resolution is sent to the customer.

## Escalation
- Disputes over account closure or frozen funds are escalated to a supervisor review.
- Suspected unauthorized account access is referred to the Fraud Investigations team.
- Repeated fee errors on the same account are escalated to a process review.

## What we do not do
- We do not refund fees that were correctly applied under the account agreement.
- We do not release funds subject to a valid legal hold or fraud investigation.
- We do not share account access or details without verified authorization.
