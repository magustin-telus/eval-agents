# id: POL-CREDIT-CARD
# title: Credit Card Complaint Resolution Policy
# category: credit_card

## Scope
Complaints involving credit card accounts: billing errors, unauthorized or fraudulent
charges, duplicate charges, chargeback disputes, interest and fee disputes, payment
posting issues, and credit limit decisions.

## Investigation steps
1. Verify the cardholder's identity and locate the disputed transaction(s).
2. Confirm whether each charge was authorized; check for duplicates and merchant errors.
3. For suspected fraud, place a temporary hold on the affected card.
4. Review payment history and statement dates relevant to the dispute.
5. Request supporting documentation from the customer where the claim is contested.

## Remediation
- Confirmed duplicate or unauthorized charges are reversed within 5 business days.
- Fraudulent charges are removed and a replacement card is issued within 7 business days.
- Erroneous interest or fees tied to a valid dispute are credited back to the account.
- A provisional credit may be applied while a complex dispute is investigated.
- A written confirmation of the resolution is sent to the customer.

## Escalation
- If the dispute is not resolved within 10 business days, escalate to a supervisor review.
- Disputes over $500 or involving suspected organized fraud are referred to the Fraud
  Investigations team.
- Unresolved regulatory or fairness concerns are referred to the Office of the Ombudsman.

## What we do not do
- We do not reverse charges that are verified as authorized and accurate.
- We do not share account details with third parties without the customer's consent.
- We do not guarantee outcomes before the investigation is complete.
