# id: POL-DEBT-COLLECTION
# title: Debt Collection Complaint Resolution Policy
# category: debt_collection

## Scope
Complaints about debt collection practices: attempts to collect a debt the customer does
not owe or already paid, incorrect debt amounts, collection contact frequency or timing,
communication at prohibited hours, third-party disclosure, and requests for debt validation.

## Investigation steps
1. Identify the debt in question and the collecting party (internal or third-party agency).
2. Verify the debt's validity, amount, and ownership against account records.
3. Review the contact history for frequency, timing, and channel of communications.
4. Check whether a valid debt-validation request was received and answered.
5. Determine whether any communication breached contact rules or disclosed information improperly.

## Remediation
- A written debt-validation notice is provided within 5 business days of a valid request.
- Collection activity is paused while a disputed debt is under validation.
- Debts confirmed as not owed or already paid are closed and recall instructions are sent
  to any third-party agency within 5 business days.
- Contact preferences and any cease-contact requests are recorded and honored.

## Escalation
- Confirmed contact-rule breaches are escalated to a compliance review.
- Disputes over debt ownership unresolved within 15 business days go to a supervisor review.
- Allegations of harassment or abusive conduct are referred to the Conduct Review team.

## What we do not do
- We do not continue collecting on a debt confirmed as invalid or already settled.
- We do not contact customers outside permitted hours or after a valid cease-contact request.
- We do not disclose debt details to unauthorized third parties.
