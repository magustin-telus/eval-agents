# id: POL-MORTGAGE-LOANS
# title: Mortgages and Loans Complaint Resolution Policy
# category: mortgages_and_loans

## Scope
Complaints about mortgages and consumer/auto/personal loans: payment posting and escrow
errors, interest rate or amortization disputes, payoff and statement issues, loan
modification and forbearance requests, servicing transfers, and foreclosure or repossession concerns.

## Investigation steps
1. Identify the loan or mortgage account and the specific issue (payment, escrow, rate, status).
2. Reconcile the payment ledger, escrow analysis, and interest calculations against records.
3. Review the loan agreement terms relevant to the dispute.
4. For hardship cases, assess eligibility for modification, forbearance, or a repayment plan.
5. Confirm the status of any servicing transfer and that records were passed correctly.

## Remediation
- Misapplied or misposted payments are corrected and the ledger is restated within 7 business days.
- Escrow miscalculations are corrected and any overage is refunded per the escrow analysis.
- A corrected payoff or account statement is issued within 7 business days of a verified error.
- Eligible hardship requests receive a written modification or forbearance offer.
- Foreclosure or repossession activity is paused while a related complaint is under review.

## Escalation
- Disputes involving foreclosure, repossession, or default are escalated to a senior loan
  specialist immediately.
- Servicing-transfer errors that lose payment history are escalated to a compliance review.
- Hardship cases denied at first review are referred to the Loss Mitigation team.

## What we do not do
- We do not change agreed contractual loan terms outside an approved modification.
- We do not waive validly owed principal or contractual interest without authorization.
- We do not guarantee approval of a modification or forbearance request.
